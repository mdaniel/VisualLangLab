This area contains output from the parser, specifically, it displays the AST
or any error-messages produced by a parser run.
Normal output is printed in black, while errors are printed in red.
The contents of this area can be copied to the clipboard by clicking the
"Copy log" button, or selecting "Log" -> "Copy log" from the main menu.
The area can be cleared by clicking the "Clear log" button,
or selecting "Log" -> "Clear log" from the main menu.
