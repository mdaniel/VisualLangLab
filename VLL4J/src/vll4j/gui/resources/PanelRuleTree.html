This area contains the rule-tree (the tree-structured graphical representation
of a parser-rule used by VisualLangLab. The rule-tree also supports
context-menus for editing the rule-tree.
Examples of rule-trees can be seen by loading any of the sample grammars
found under "Help" -> "Sample grammars" -> ...
Further details can be found at http://vll.java.net/EditingTheGrammarTree.html
